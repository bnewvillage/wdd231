GP-R7 1PC Leather Suit

Optimized for trackday, racing, and advanced road riding, this suit is crafted from race-grade 1.3mm bovine leather for superior abrasion resistance, flexibility, and protection. Its race fit features extensive perforations for airflow and an integrated aero hump designed to work seamlessly with the Supertech R10 helmet.

Key Features:

Material & Fit:

  • 1.3mm race-spec leather with dual-layer reinforcement in critical areas.
  • Ergonomic race fit with pre-curved arms, legs, and multi-directional leather accordions.

Protection:

  • CE Level 2 Nucleon PLASMA Pro armor on elbows, shoulders, knees, and shins.
  • Integrated GP DFS shoulder/knee armor and replaceable knee sliders.
  • Optional chest and full-back protectors available.

Aerodynamics & Ventilation:

  • Supertech R10 aero hump for streamlined performance.
  • Extensive perforations across the torso, thighs, and arms.
  • Airbag system compatibility.

Additional Details:

  • Kevlar grip zone on the knee for improved contact.
  • Zippered, detachable comfort liner for off-bike comfort.
